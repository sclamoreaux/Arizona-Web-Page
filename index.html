Explore Diverse Landscapes...

Southern Arizona is encompassed by the Sonoran Desert, the epitome of rugged beauty, rich with unique flora and fauna. The city of Tucson is situated between four fantastic mountain ranges, offering countless opportunities for outdoor adventure. Explore majestic Saguaro cactus forests in Sabino Canyon, or escape to the cool pine-scented air in the forests of Mount Lemmon above.

Central Arizona includes the Phoenix area, where there’s no shortage of things to do. Southeast of the city, check out the jagged peaks of the Superstition mountains. A short drive north brings you to one of the most gorgeous locales in America: Sedona. Boasting breathtaking red rock formations fringed with green juniper trees, Sedona’s magical landscape is the must-see jewel of Arizona.

Northern Arizona is home to our most popular attraction and one of Earth’s greatest natural wonders, the Grand Canyon. Take a hike along the rim for the jaw-dropping views. For water recreation, venture over to Lake Powell, which winds through Glen Canyon, extending into Utah. A few hours’ drive south, you’ll encounter Flagstaff, a mountain town nestled amongst ponderosa pine forests.
